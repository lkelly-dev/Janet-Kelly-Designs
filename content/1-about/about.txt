KellyDesigns is an interior and landscape design firm that specializes in residential projects. Known for her eclectic style, Janet loves to merge the beloved classics with contemporary elements to create clean, calming spaces. She has the ability to walk in and immediately visualize the appropriate changes for a space, its potential and its limitations.  Janet offers real life solutions tailored to the client's individual tastes and desires. By utilizing a professional, you will see a fresh perspective and realize the true potential of your home.
